    • About The Sharks

    The Sharks, based in Durban, South Africa, are a prominent professional rugby team with a rich history. Originally established in 1995 during the advent of professionalism in rugby, the Sharks have been a formidable force in both domestic and international competitions. Competing in the United Rugby Championship and Super Rugby, the team has showcased its prowess with numerous playoff appearances. Known for their fierce and dynamic playing style, the Sharks boast a strong fan base and have produced talented players who have donned the Springbok jersey, contributing to South Africa's rugby success. With a commitment to excellence and a legacy that spans decades, the Sharks continue to make waves in the rugby seas, leaving an indelible mark on the sport's landscape.

    The Hollywoodbets Sharks: A Rugby Legacy

    The Hollywoodbets Sharks, proudly representing Durban in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa, are a powerhouse in professional rugby. Competing globally in the United Rugby Championship and EPCR Challenge Cup, they were a formidable force in Super Rugby until 2020. Boasting a rich history, the team has reached the Super Rugby final four times but has yet to secure the title. Home matches unfold at the iconic Hollywoodbets Kings Park Stadium in Durban, creating an electric atmosphere for fans.

    Sharks' Super Rugby Journey: Triumphs and Challenges

    Navigating the complex waters of Super Rugby, the Sharks have left an indelible mark. From their inaugural representation in 1993 to becoming runners-up in 1994, the team consistently showcased top-tier rugby. Noteworthy players like Thomas du Toit, Makazole Mapimpi, and Lukhanyo Am, among others, have elevated the Sharks to international acclaim. Despite facing challenges, including a heart-wrenching loss in the 2007 final, the Sharks have continuously evolved, embodying resilience and determination.

    The Sharks Brand: Marketing Mastery

    In a bold move in 1995, Natal embraced a new identity—the Sharks. Spearheaded by Terry Kukle, the branding strategy faced initial resistance but quickly became a marketing triumph. Sharkie, the mascot, debuted, and the brand's success extended beyond the rugby field. Widely recognized and internationally acclaimed, the Sharks' marketing strategy stands as a case study in success, elevating the team to one of the most beloved and well-supported unions in South Africa.

    Hollywoodbets Sharks in Transition: A New Era

    As the rugby landscape evolves, the Hollywoodbets Sharks embrace change. In 2021, an American consortium, MVM Holdings, partnered with Roc Nation, JayZ's entertainment agency, acquiring a 51% majority stake. This strategic move ushers in a new era, signaling the Sharks' global appeal. With eyes set on the future, the team competes in the United Rugby Championship, continuing to captivate fans worldwide with its dynamic play and enduring legacy.
