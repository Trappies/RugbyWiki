    • About San Diego Legion

    The San Diego Legion is a dynamic professional rugby union team based in San Diego, California, and a proud member of Major League Rugby (MLR). Founded in 2017, the Legion swiftly asserted themselves as a formidable force in the league. Known for their strong commitment to excellence, the team has garnered a passionate fan base. Over the seasons, the Legion has consistently displayed skillful and entertaining rugby, solidifying their presence as a key contender in the MLR. With a vibrant and competitive spirit, the San Diego Legion continues to contribute to the growth and excitement of professional rugby in the United States.

    San Diego Legion: Powerhouse in California Rugby

    Founded in 2017, the San Diego Legion is a prominent American rugby team competing in Major League Rugby (MLR). With a rich history, the Legion has become a stalwart in the vibrant rugby community of California.

    Home Field: Snapdragon Stadium

    The Legion proudly calls Snapdragon Stadium its home, situated on the San Diego State University campus in Mission Valley. Boasting a seating capacity of 35,000, Snapdragon Stadium is a hub of sports, hosting not only rugby but also serving as the home ground for San Diego State Aztecs football and San Diego Wave FC.

    Navigating Challenges: Relocation Amidst COVID-19

    In response to the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, the Legion temporarily relocated to Las Vegas, Nevada, for the 2021 season. Despite the adversity, the team demonstrated resilience and returned to San Diego on April 12, 2021, ready to continue their pursuit of excellence.

    Broadcasting Excellence: Bringing Rugby to Fans

    The Legion ensures that fans stay connected by broadcasting select 2021 home and away MLR games locally on FOX 5 San Diego. Additionally, the team leverages digital platforms, streaming live on ESPN+ and archiving games on their YouTube channel. The dynamic commentary duo of Matt McCarthy and Marc Sterchbina adds to the thrilling spectator experience.
