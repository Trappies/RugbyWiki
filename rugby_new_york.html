    • About Rugby New York

    Rugby United New York, commonly known as Rugby New York, stands as a prominent professional rugby union team in Major League Rugby (MLR), based in the captivating city that never sleeps. Established as part of MLR's expansion, Rugby New York entered the league for the 2019 season, becoming a dynamic force in American rugby. The team has been instrumental in fostering the growth of the sport in the New York metropolitan area, attracting a passionate fan base. With a commitment to excellence on and off the field, Rugby New York has become a central figure in the vibrant rugby community, showcasing the grit and determination synonymous with the city it proudly represents.

    Rugby New York's Historic Triumph at MLR Final

    In 2022, Rugby New York, affectionately known as the Ironworkers, achieved a significant milestone in their first season under the new name. Making their inaugural Major League Rugby (MLR) Final appearance, the team secured a championship title in front of a fervent crowd at Red Bull Arena in nearby Harrison, New Jersey. The victory marked a crowning achievement for the team and solidified their status as a force to be reckoned with in the MLR.

    Foundation and Leadership by James Kennedy

    Rugby New York, previously named Rugby United New York, traces its roots to its foundation in April 2018 by James Kennedy, the owner of Murphy Kennedy Group, a prominent construction management company in New York. Kennedy's leadership has been instrumental in steering the team toward success, emphasizing a commitment to excellence both on and off the field.

    Inaugural Season and Home Field at MCU Park

    In its inaugural season, Rugby United New York, under the guidance of head coach Mike Tolkin, showcased its prowess in the exhibition matches against notable opponents. The team played home matches at MCU Park in Brooklyn during their first full season, creating a vibrant and engaging atmosphere for fans and players alike.

    Home Field Shift to Memorial Field in Mount Vernon, NY

    In a strategic move, Rugby New York announced in January 2023 that Memorial Field in Mount Vernon, NY, would become their new home field. This decision reflects the team's commitment to connecting with local communities and enhancing the rugby experience for their dedicated fan base.
