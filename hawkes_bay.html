    • About Hawkes Bay

    The Hawke's Bay rugby team, often referred to as the Hawke's Bay Magpies, is a prominent New Zealand rugby union team with a rich history. Established in the Hawke's Bay region, the team has been a formidable force in the National Provincial Championship since its inception in 1976. The Magpies play their home games at McLean Park in Napier, creating a spirited atmosphere for fans. Affiliated with the Hurricanes Super Rugby franchise, the team has been a breeding ground for talented players who often progress to the national stage. With distinctive black and white colors, the Hawke's Bay Magpies embody the region's rugby heritage and continue to be a competitive force in New Zealand rugby.

    Historic Foundation

    The Hawke's Bay rugby union team, affectionately known as the Magpies, boasts a storied history dating back to its establishment in 1884. This rich foundation has laid the groundwork for their continued success in New Zealand's rugby landscape.

    Provincial Powerhouse

    As a formidable force in New Zealand rugby, the Hawke's Bay Magpies have embraced the challenge of the National Provincial Championship since its inception in 1976. Their competitive spirit and commitment to excellence make them a perennial contender in this prestigious competition.

    McLean Park Fortress

    The Magpies create a fortress at McLean Park in Napier, their home ground in the Hawke's Bay region. With passionate fans rallying behind them, the team transforms McLean Park into a formidable venue where victories are celebrated and opponents face a tough challenge.

    Hurricanes Affiliation

    Affiliated with the Hurricanes Super Rugby franchise, the Magpies serve as a talent pool for emerging rugby stars. This collaboration strengthens the rugby ecosystem, fostering growth and providing a pathway for players to shine on the national and international stages.

    Iconic Black and White

    The Hawke's Bay Magpies proudly don the iconic black and white colors, symbolizing the heritage and traditions of the region. These colors embody the team's commitment to excellence, creating a visual representation of Hawke's Bay's rugby legacy.
