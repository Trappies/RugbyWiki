    • About Rugby ATL

    Rugby ATL stands as a prominent professional rugby union team based in Atlanta, Georgia, competing with distinction in Major League Rugby (MLR). The team was established to represent the vibrant rugby culture in the southeastern United States. Since its inception, Rugby ATL has become a powerhouse in the MLR, showcasing exceptional talent and fostering a strong sense of community. With a commitment to the growth of rugby in the region, Rugby ATL continues to make waves in the league, captivating fans with its skillful play and unwavering spirit.

    Inception and Identity

    Rugby ATL, a professional rugby union team, emerged on the Major League Rugby (MLR) scene in 2020, bringing a new era of rugby to Atlanta. The team's journey began with its inclusion in the league's expansion plans announced on September 21, 2018. The distinctive name "Rugby ATL" was unveiled on February 26, 2019, setting the stage for a brand that would soon make its mark on the MLR landscape.

    Relocation Announcement

    On August 2, 2023, Major League Rugby dropped a bombshell, announcing that Rugby ATL would be relocating for the 2024 season. The destination remained shrouded in mystery, leaving fans and the rugby community speculating about the team's new home. The decision hinted at a significant chapter in Rugby ATL's story, sparking anticipation and discussions among supporters.

    New Horizons in Los Angeles

    The suspense surrounding Rugby ATL's relocation came to an end on August 17, as it was officially declared that the team would find its new home in Los Angeles. This marked a bold move for the franchise, embracing a fresh start and a connection to the vibrant rugby culture in the City of Angels. The announcement ushered in a wave of excitement and curiosity about how Rugby ATL would integrate into the rugby landscape of Los Angeles for the upcoming season.
