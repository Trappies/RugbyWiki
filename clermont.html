    • About Clermont

    Clermont Auvergne, commonly known as ASM Clermont Auvergne, stands as a rugby powerhouse in the French league system. Founded in 1911, the club is based in Clermont-Ferrand in the Auvergne region. Renowned for their passionate fanbase, the team plays home matches at the Parc des Sports Marcel-Michelin, where the electric atmosphere adds to the formidable challenge for visiting teams. Clermont has consistently been a top contender in French rugby, claiming the French top-division championship in 1925, 1936, 1956, 1966, and 2010. The club's distinctive yellow and blue jerseys symbolize their rich heritage and the unwavering support of their dedicated fans. With a storied history and a commitment to excellence, Clermont Auvergne continues to be a dominant force in French rugby, captivating audiences with their thrilling performances and competitive spirit.

    ASM Clermont Auvergne: A Storied Legacy in French Rugby

    Association Sportive Montferrandaise Clermont Auvergne (ASM Clermont Auvergne) embodies a rich rugby legacy. Established in 1911, the club persevered through numerous challenges, reaching thirteen French Championship finals before clinching the title in the 2009-10 and 2016-17 seasons. The iconic yellow and blue jerseys, inspired by Michelin's colors, symbolize the team's resilience and triumph.

    Stade Marcel-Michelin - The Bib Park Experience

    The Stade Marcel-Michelin, affectionately known as The Bib Park, serves as the formidable fortress for ASM Clermont Auvergne. With a seating capacity of 19,022, this iconic stadium creates an electrifying atmosphere for fans. Witness the roaring cheers, the breathtaking moments, and the unwavering support that make every match a memorable experience.

    Vern Cotter Era - A Resurgence in Excellence

    The Vern Cotter era marked a resurgence in ASM Clermont Auvergne's excellence. Taking over in 2006, Cotter guided the team to their first final since 2001, winning the European Challenge Cup in 2007. Under his leadership, the club consistently competed at the highest levels, showcasing resilience and determination on their journey to reclaiming the French Championship.

    European Glory and Heartbreaks

    ASM Clermont Auvergne's European campaigns have been a rollercoaster of glory and heartbreaks. From winning the European Challenge Cup in 2007 to reaching the Heineken Cup final in 2013, the team has left an indelible mark. Each campaign, filled with memorable clashes, has added to the club's storied history in European rugby.

    Cultural Exchange with New Orleans Gold

    In January 2020, ASM Clermont Auvergne expanded its reach by acquiring a minority stake in the American rugby club, New Orleans Gold. Beyond player exchanges, this partnership aims to facilitate cultural exchanges between Louisiana and France. Witnessing the fusion of rugby traditions and shared values, this collaboration adds a global dimension to ASM Clermont Auvergne's identity.

    ASM Clermont Auvergne - A Legacy Unfolding

    ASM Clermont Auvergne's enduring spirit is woven into the fabric of French rugby history. From its early days as AS Michelin to the present as a force to be reckoned with, the club's legacy unfolds with each match. The journey, marked by triumphs, challenges, and unwavering support, continues to shape the story of ASM Clermont Auvergne.
