    • About The Chicago Hounds

    The Chicago Hounds are a professional rugby union team in Major League Rugby (MLR) based in Chicagoland. The team was founded in 2022 and is co-owned by a small group of investors, including a minority share by local residents Matt Satchwell and Phil Groves. Their home stadium is SeatGeek Stadium in Bridgeview, Illinois. Games are broadcast on Marquee Sports Network.

    Chicago Hounds in Major League Rugby

    The Chicago Hounds, established in 2022, proudly represent Chicagoland in Major League Rugby (MLR). Co-owned by a select group of investors, with Matt Satchwell and Phil Groves holding minority shares, the team calls SeatGeek Stadium in Bridgeview, Illinois, its home ground. Fans can catch the exciting action as the Hounds' games are broadcast on Marquee Sports Network.

    Inaugural Season and Rivalry Highlights

    Joining MLR as the newest expansion franchise in 2023, the Chicago Hounds quickly made their mark in the Western Conference. Despite finishing 5th out of 6 teams in the conference and 9th out of 12 teams overall, the Hounds showcased their competitive spirit. Notably, their inaugural season featured a spirited rivalry with the Dallas Jackals, resulting in two victories for the Hounds in the celebrated Dog Bowl matches.

    Home Turf Advantage at SeatGeek Stadium

    SeatGeek Stadium in Bridgeview, Illinois, serves as the vibrant battleground for the Chicago Hounds. With its state-of-the-art facilities and passionate fanbase, the stadium provides the perfect backdrop for the Hounds to exhibit their rugby prowess. As the team's fortress, SeatGeek Stadium is where the Hounds aim to create memorable moments and secure victories in the thrilling landscape of Major League Rugby.
