    • About Nola Gold

    NOLA Gold is a professional rugby union team competing in Major League Rugby (MLR) and is based in New Orleans, Louisiana. Established in 2017, the team has become a prominent force in American rugby. The Gold plays its home matches at the Shrine on Airline stadium. Known for its vibrant fanbase and commitment to developing rugby in the region, NOLA Gold has steadily made its mark in the MLR since joining the league. With a strong presence in the Southern Conference, the team continues to contribute to the growth and popularity of rugby in the United States.

    New Orleans Gold Overview

    The New Orleans Gold, known as NOLA Gold, is a professional rugby union team founded in 2017 and competes in Major League Rugby. Since January 2020, they have had a minority stake owned by the French rugby powerhouse ASM Clermont Auvergne.

    Home Field - Gold Mine on Airline

    NOLA Gold plays its home matches at the Gold Mine on Airline, situated in Metairie, Louisiana. Formerly known as the Shrine on Airline and Zephyr Field, this venue on the East Bank of the New Orleans metropolitan area serves as the team's vibrant home ground.

    Previous Venue - Joe Zimmerman Stadium

    In the initial two seasons of Major League Rugby (2018-2019), NOLA Gold played at Joe Zimmerman Stadium in Archbishop Shaw High School. Branded as Gold Stadium, this venue on the West Bank at Marrero, Louisiana, boasted a natural turf field and grandstand seating for up to 3,000 spectators.

    Broadcasts and On-Air Talent

    Home games of NOLA Gold are broadcasted on Cox Sports Television, featuring on-air talent Ian McNulty and Geoffrey Ormsby. The broadcasts bring the thrilling rugby action to fans, enhancing the team's reach and engagement.

    Sponsorship - "GOLDen Ale" Partnership

    In the 2022 season, NOLA Gold entered into a partnership with local Louisiana business, Port Orleans Brewing Company, to create "GOLDen Ale." These 16-ounce cans are available for purchase in the brewery's taproom and on tap at the Shrine on Airline, adding a unique touch to the fan experience.
