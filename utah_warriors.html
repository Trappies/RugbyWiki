    • About Utah Warriors

    The Utah Warriors stand as a prominent professional rugby union team based in Salt Lake City, Utah, competing in Major League Rugby (MLR). Established in 2017, the Warriors have become a formidable force in the league, consistently showcasing their prowess on the rugby field. The team's commitment to excellence has been evident in their competitive spirit and contributions to the growth of rugby in the United States. With a dedicated fan base and a strong presence in the MLR, the Utah Warriors continue to play a pivotal role in elevating the standard of rugby in the region.

    Utah Warriors Rugby Excellence

    The Utah Warriors stand as a powerhouse in Major League Rugby, showcasing their dedication to rugby excellence. Established in 2010 and re-founded in 2017, the team has been a stalwart in the league, contributing significantly to the growth of rugby in the United States.

    Zions Bank Stadium

    The Utah Warriors proudly call Zions Bank Stadium in Herriman, Utah, their home ground. With a seating capacity of 5,000, this state-of-the-art venue provides an electrifying atmosphere for fans to witness the Warriors' on-field prowess. The adjacent training facility, featuring two full-sized indoor turf fields, underscores their commitment to top-tier performance.

    Dynamic Broadcasting

    In 2019, Utah Warriors' home games took center stage on KMYU, a CBS affiliate station owned by the Sinclair Broadcast Group. The broadcast, led by the dynamic duo of Jarom Jordan and Jonny Linehan, brought the thrilling Warriors' matches to fans across the region, further solidifying their presence in the rugby landscape.
