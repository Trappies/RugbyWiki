    • About The New England Free Jacks

    The New England Free Jacks, a prominent Major League Rugby (MLR) team, have established themselves as a force to be reckoned with in the American rugby scene. Founded in 2018, the team is based in Boston, Massachusetts, and competes in the top-tier MLR, showcasing the growth and popularity of rugby in the United States. With a commitment to fostering local talent and embracing the rich rugby traditions of New England, the Free Jacks have rapidly become a beloved entity in the region. Their home matches are played at the iconic Veterans Memorial Stadium in Quincy, providing a vibrant and passionate setting for fans to support their team. The Free Jacks' dedication to community engagement, combined with their on-field prowess, positions them as a key player in the evolution of rugby in the New England region and beyond.

    New England Free Jacks: MLR Champions 2023

    The New England Free Jacks, a professional rugby union team in Major League Rugby (MLR) based in Greater Boston, achieved a remarkable feat on July 8, 2023. They secured the title of MLR Champions for the 2023 season, triumphing over the San Diego Legion in a closely contested match.

    Inauguration and Early Matches

    Founded in September 2018, the New England Free Jacks swiftly entered professional competition in October of the same year. Their official participation in MLR began in the 2020 season. The team's inaugural match took place on October 20, 2018, against the Ontario Arrows at Wanderers Grounds in Halifax, Nova Scotia. Early rosters featured a blend of experienced professionals from the disbanded PRO Rugby competition and talented local players from clubs like the Boston Irish Wolfhounds and Mystic River.

    NFL Influence and Ownership

    Notably, the Free Jacks boast minority ownership by NFL players Nate Ebner and Patrick Chung. This infusion of diverse sporting expertise enhances the team's profile and contributes to their success on the rugby field. The Free Jacks' journey from inception to MLR Champions underscores their rapid rise and competitive prowess in the league.
