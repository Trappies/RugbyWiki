    • About Counties Manukau

    Established in 1955, the Counties Manukau rugby team embodies the rich rugby culture of New Zealand. Competing in the National Provincial Championship (NPC), now branded as the Bunnings NPC, the team has showcased remarkable determination and skill over the years. Their home matches are played at Navigation Homes Stadium in Pukekohe, creating a vibrant atmosphere for fans. Counties Manukau has faced formidable opponents, leaving an indelible mark on the rugby landscape. As they continue to forge ahead, the team remains a testament to the passion and talent that define New Zealand's rugby tradition.

    A Rugby Powerhouse in Black, White, and Red

    Founded in 1955, the Counties Manukau Steelers stand as a rugby institution in New Zealand. Competing in the National Provincial Championship (NPC), they showcase a legacy steeped in tradition and success. The Navigation Homes Stadium in Pukekohe, Auckland, becomes a battleground where the Steelers exhibit their prowess in distinctive black, white, and red jerseys. Affiliated with the Chiefs Super Rugby franchise, this team continues to be a force, representing the heart and soul of New Zealand rugby.

    The Iconic Home of Counties Manukau Rugby

    Step into the heart of New Zealand rugby at Navigation Homes Stadium in Pukekohe, Auckland, where the Counties Manukau Steelers command the field. This iconic stadium, witnessing the birth of countless rugby legends, becomes a battleground for the Steelers' intense clashes. With a seating capacity of thousands, fans clad in black, white, and red rally behind their team, creating an electric atmosphere that defines the spirit of Counties Manukau rugby.

    Chiefs Super Rugby Affiliation: Counties Manukau's Path to Glory

    Counties Manukau Steelers, donned in their distinctive black, white, and red jerseys, proudly align themselves with the Chiefs Super Rugby franchise. This affiliation not only enhances their prowess on the field but also solidifies their status as a rugby powerhouse. As they compete in the reformed National Provincial Championship, the Steelers carry the hopes and legacy of two formidable rugby entities, showcasing a commitment to excellence and a vibrant tradition that spans decades.
