    • About Tasman Makos

    The Tasman Makos, a formidable New Zealand rugby union team, call the picturesque Tasman region home. Established as a union in recent times, the Makos have swiftly carved a niche for themselves in the rugby landscape. Donning the colors of green, black, and gold, the team competes with zeal in the National Provincial Championship. The Makos' prowess is not only a testament to their on-field skills but also reflects the rugby passion that resonates in the Tasman community. Affiliated with the Crusaders Super Rugby franchise, the Tasman Makos embody the spirit of excellence and unity in New Zealand rugby.

    Tasman Mako: Rugby Excellence in Te Tai-o-Aorere

    Founded in 2006, the Tasman Mako, formerly known as the Tasman Makos, stand as a powerhouse in New Zealand's rugby scene. Competing in the National Provincial Championship, this professional rugby union team hails from Tasman Bay / Te Tai-o-Aorere. With home games hosted at Lansdowne Park in Blenheim or Trafalgar Park in Nelson, the Mako showcase their skills in the vibrant Tasman region. Affiliated with the Crusaders Super Rugby franchise, their red and blue colors symbolize the passion and determination embedded in the Tasman Mako's rugby legacy.

    Home Turf Glory: Lansdowne Park and Trafalgar Park

    Experience the thrill of rugby at its finest as the Tasman Mako dominate their home games at Lansdowne Park in Blenheim or Trafalgar Park in Nelson. These iconic venues in the Tasman region serve as the battlegrounds where the Mako unleash their prowess in the National Provincial Championship. Be part of the fervent atmosphere as the team, affiliated with the Crusaders Super Rugby franchise, defends their turf in striking red and blue, leaving an indelible mark on New Zealand's rugby legacy.
