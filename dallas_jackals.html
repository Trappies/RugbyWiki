    • About The Dallas Jackals

    The Dallas Jackals, a prominent team in Major League Rugby (MLR), entered the league as an expansion franchise with high ambitions. Founded in 2020, the team boasts a diverse ownership group, including a minority share held by local residents eager to support rugby growth in the region. The Jackals' home matches unfold at the renowned Globe Life Park in Arlington, Texas, providing fans with a dynamic venue to witness their favorite team in action. As they strive to make their mark in MLR, the Dallas Jackals are committed to delivering exciting rugby and fostering a strong connection with their passionate fanbase.

    Dallas Jackals Debut in MLR

    The Dallas Jackals, a burgeoning Major League Rugby (MLR) team, embarked on their inaugural season in 2022 as an expansion franchise. Originally slated to join MLR in 2021, the Jackals, led by an investment group featuring Neil Leibman of the Texas Rangers and Top Tier Sports, made a strategic decision to postpone their debut to 2022. The team proudly represents Arlington, Texas, and Choctaw Stadium serves as their home ground, providing a vibrant venue for rugby enthusiasts. Notably, on July 21, 2022, the Jackals announced Santiago Sodini as their new general manager, signaling a strategic move to strengthen the team's management and position in MLR.
